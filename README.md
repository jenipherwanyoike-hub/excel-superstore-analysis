# excel-superstore-analysis

## Tools Used
Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Formulas)

## Dataset
Sample Superstore Dataset — 9,994 orders across 4 years

## Business Questions Answered
- Which product category is most profitable?
- Which region generates the most sales?
- Which customers are most valuable?
- What percentage of orders are unprofitable?

## Key Findings
- Technology is the highest revenue category ($836K) and most profitable ($145K)
- Furniture has high sales ($742K) but low profit — Tables sub-category loses $17K
- 19% of all orders are unprofitable, resulting in $156K total losses
- Q4 is consistently the strongest quarter across all years
- Sean Miller is the highest spending customer ($25K) but generates a $1,980 loss
